WHAT'S AN QUANTOVA WALLET?

Quantova wallets are applications that give you control over your account. Just like your physical wallet, it contains everything you need to prove your identity and handle your assets. Your wallet allows you to sign in to applications, read your balance, send transactions and verify your identity.

Wallets are what most people use to handle their digital assets and identity.

Your wallet is a tool for interacting with your Quantova account. That means you can swap wallet providers at any time. Many wallets also let you manage several Quantova accounts from one application.
